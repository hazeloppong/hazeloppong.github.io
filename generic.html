Welcome to my Twi Interpreting Services. Services offered include the following: Video Interpreting, Telephone Interpreting, and Face-to-Face Interpreting.

About the Twi Language Twi, also known as Akan, is a widely spoken African language primarily used in Ghana. With an estimated 17 to 18 million Twi speakers worldwide, it is a crucial language for effective communication in Ghana and beyond. In fact, 80% of Ghanaians speak Twi as their first or second language. The Twi language consists of several dialects, including Akuapim Twi, Fante Twi, Ashanti Twi, and Bono Twi. As a professional interpreter, I have an in-depth understanding of the different dialects and cultural nuances of the Twi language. With my native fluency and cultural knowledge, I provide accurate and culturally relevant interpretations every time.
